The Saint Cloud Dam is located on the Sauk River in Saint Cloud, Minnesota. It is a concrete arch dam with a height of 134 feet (41 m). The dam impounds 660 acre-feet (1,500 m3) of water and has a capacity of 1,720 acre-feet (3,000 m3).
