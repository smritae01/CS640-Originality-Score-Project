Morgan Creek Entertainment was founded in 1989 by film producers Joel and Ethan Coen, who are also the company's co-CEOs. The company is based in New York City.

Morgan Creek has produced or co-produced films including "The Big Lebowski", "O Brother, Where Art Thou?", "No Country for Old Men", "The Blind Side", "The Help" and "Suicide Squad". The company has also produced television series including "Breaking Bad", "Boardwalk Empire", "Downton Abbey", "Game of Thrones" and "Veep".
