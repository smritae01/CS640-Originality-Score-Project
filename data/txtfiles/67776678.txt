Helichrysum calvertianum is a perennial herb with a stem up to 60 centimeters long. The leaves are linear to lance-shaped, green to blue-green, and almost hairless. The inflorescence is a large array of many white or pinkish flowers heads, each up to 10 centimeters wide. The fruit is a capsule about 1 centimeter long.
