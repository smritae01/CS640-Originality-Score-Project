a German professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing with the Hannover Scorpions in the Deutsche Eishockey Liga (DEL).

Vogel was drafted by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the first round, sixth overall, of the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. He played with the Penguins for four seasons before being traded to the Anaheim Ducks in 2006. He spent two seasons with the Ducks before being traded to the Columbus Blue Jackets in 2008. He played with Columbus for one season before being traded to the Nashville Predators in 2009.

Vogel signed a contract with Hannover Scorpions on July 1, 2013.
