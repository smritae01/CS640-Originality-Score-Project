thriller film written and directed by R. Sarathkumar. Produced by Sarath Kumar and A. R. Rahman, the film stars Sarathkumar, Shruti Haasan, and Ramji Rao Iyengar in lead roles. It is a remake of the Hindi film Darr (2013), which was also written and directed by Sarathkumar.

The film tells the story of a retired police officer, Remo (Sarathkumar), who is asked to help investigate a series of murders that are related to an ancient Hindu religious text.
