The story follows the journey of six young people who are chosen to become idols and travel to a different city every week to perform for the public. However, their dreams of becoming famous quickly turn into a nightmare when they are forced to live in a TV studio and are systematically exploited by the network responsible for their careers.

The series was first announced in February 2016, with the first episode airing on October 7 of that year. The second episode aired on October 14, and the remaining episodes were released weekly thereafter. The series has been licensed in North America by Aniplex of America, who released the complete series on home video in December 2017.
