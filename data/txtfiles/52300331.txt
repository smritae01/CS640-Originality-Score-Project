Rogers was born in Bakersfield, California, to Sinclair and Janie Rogers. He had two older brothers, Mark and Skip. In 1974, he married his high school sweetheart, Lynda. They had one daughter, Amber.

Rogers died of a heroin overdose in 1998 at age 43.
