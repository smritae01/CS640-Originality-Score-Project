The French battleship Suffren was one of the most advanced warships of its time when she was built in 1904. She was designed by naval architect Auguste Lamarre and was the first battleship built in France. Suffren served throughout World War I and fought in several significant battles, notably the Battle of Jutland in 1916. After the war, Suffren served as a training ship before being decommissioned in 1927. She was sold for breaking up in 1936.
