It was the 37th edition of the Peach Bowl, and featured the North Carolina Tar Heels of the Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC) against the Texas Tech Red Raiders of the Big 12 Conference.

The Tar Heels entered the game as 10-point underdogs, but upset Texas Tech 31-24 to earn their fourth bowl win in six years. North Carolina quarterback T. J. Yates threw for a career-high 422 yards and four touchdowns, while running back Lorenzo White rushed for 131 yards and two scores.
