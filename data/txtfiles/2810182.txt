Mount Stephenson is located in the Queen Maud Mountains, which are a subrange of the Ostrobothnia Mountains. The mountain is east of Mount Scopus and north of Mount Hadley. The nearest town is Hays Bay, which is located about 20 kilometers (12 mi) south of the mountain.

The mountain was first sighted by a British expedition under the leadership of James Clark Ross on January 24, 1842. The first ascent was made by members of the British Antarctic Expedition under Robert Falcon Scott on December 14, 1912.
