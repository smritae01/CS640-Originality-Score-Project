Walawalkar was born on 27 December 1897 in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra to a Marathi-speaking family. He had his early education at a Jesuit school in Ratnagiri. He then studied at the University of Bombay, where he obtained an M.A. in 1914 and a B.A. in 1916. After graduating from the university, Walawalkar worked as an assistant professor at the Marathi language department of Bombay University from 1916 to 1923.

Walawalkar travelled extensively during this period, visiting Europe and North America. His first book of poems, "Taranga", was published in 1927 and was well received by critics. In 1938, he married Hema Malini, with whom he had two children.
