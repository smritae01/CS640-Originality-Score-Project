Telugu-language action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli and produced by K. Raghavendra Rao. It is the fourth film in the Baahubali series, and the sequel to the 2010 film Baahubali: The Beginning. The film stars Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty and Tamannaah Bhatia in lead roles with Sathyaraj, Ramya Krishnan, and Nathalie Chauhan in supporting roles.

The film tells the story of the battle between two brothers for the throne of a kingdom. The elder brother (Prabhas) is a powerful warrior who has been training for years to take on his brother (Rana Daggubati). When their father (Sathyaraj) dies, the brothers fight for control of the kingdom.
