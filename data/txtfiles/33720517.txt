Formed in 2006 in Los Angeles, California, Patent Pending is an American rock band led by John Feldmann. The band has released three studio albums to date, including their most recent release, "Dirty Gold" (2013). 

The band's sound has been described as "lo-fi post-punk", with elements of garage rock, funk and electronica. Their music has been compared to the likes of the Pixies, the Strokes and the White Stripes.
