Alexander was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets with the seventh overall pick in the 2000 NBA draft. He has also played for the Milwaukee Bucks, Boston Celtics, Miami Heat, Minnesota Timberwolves, Philadelphia 76ers, Detroit Pistons and Dallas Mavericks. He has won championships with the Heat in 2006 and 2007. Alexander was named to the All-Defensive Second Team in 2006 and 2007.

On July 10, 2013, Alexander signed with the Russian team BC Khimki. In February 2014, Alexander left Khimki and signed with Spanish team CB Gran Canaria. On September 2, 2014, Alexander returned to Khimki.
