Sephus received her Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Utah in 1984, and her Doctor of Philosophy degree from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1988. She joined the faculty at Stanford University in 2002. Sephus's research focuses on artificial intelligence and machine learning, with an emphasis on applications in health care and finance.
