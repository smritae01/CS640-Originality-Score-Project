Dimo Hamaambo was born in Ruhengeri, Rwanda, on 18 March 1944.
He attended primary and secondary school in Rwanda before studying at the University of Ottawa, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1967.

Dimo Hamaambo joined the Rwandan Army in 1967 and fought in the Rwandan Civil War. 
He became a lieutenant colonel in 1985 and a colonel in 1989. 
From 1985 to 1989, Dimo Hamaambo served as Chief of Staff of the Rwandan Armed Forces. 
In 1989, he was appointed Deputy Chief of Staff for Operations at the Ministry of Defence. 
From 1990 to 1994, Dimo Hamaambo served as Chief of Staff for the Rwandan Armed Forces.
