The Women's Flat Track Derby Association Apprentice Program was created to provide novice skaters with the opportunity to develop their skills and learn about the sport of flat track roller derby. The Apprentice Program provides skaters with opportunities to participate in practice and games, as well as educational opportunities. 

The Women's Flat Track Derby Association Apprentice Program is open to any skater who is at least 14 years old and has not previously competed in a WFTDA sanctioned bout. Applications for the Apprentice Program are accepted on a rolling basis, and new applications are accepted every month. If you are interested in applying for the Apprentice Program, please visit our website or contact us.
