Tamil-language romantic drama film directed by K. S. Ravikumar and produced by N. R. Pandian. The film stars Rajinikanth and Aishwarya Rai in the lead roles, with Murali Mohan, Ramakrishna, and Sathyaraj appearing in supporting roles. The film's story revolves around a man who falls in love with a woman who is already married to someone else.

The film was a commercial success, receiving positive reviews from critics. It was dubbed into Telugu as "Vishnuvardhanudu" and into Malayalam as "Chandhavanum Pilgrim".

Maya (Aishwarya Rai) is married to Kannadasan (Murali Mohan). They have two children, but Maya is unhappy in her marriage. One day, she meets Rajinikanth (Rajinikanth) while he is driving his car and is immediately attracted to him. She starts following him everywhere and eventually makes a move on him, but he rebuffs her.
