born 1 January 1956) is a Pakistani politician who has been a Member of the National Assembly of Pakistan since August 2018.

Awan was born on 1 January 1956 in Faisalabad, Punjab. He received his education from the Government College Faisalabad.

Awan has worked as an administrator and journalist. He served as the editor of the "Jang" newspaper from 1989 to 1997. He also served as the provincial president of the Pakistan Muslim League (N) from 2002 to 2007.

Awan was elected to the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab as a candidate of the Pakistan Muslim League (N) from Constituency PP-247 (Faisalabad-III) in Pakistani general election, 2008. He was re-elected to the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab as a candidate of the Pakistan Muslim League (N) from Constituency PP-247 (Faisalabad-III) in Pakistani general election, 2013.
