Pheretima praepinguis is a giant earthworm that can grow up to 4 feet long and weigh up to a pound. It is the largest worm in North America. This worm is native to eastern North America, where it is found in forests, meadows, and agricultural fields. Pheretima praepinguis feeds on soil particles and organic material, and can help improve soil fertility by breaking down organic matter.
