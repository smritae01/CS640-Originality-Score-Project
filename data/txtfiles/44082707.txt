Ranee Jayamaha was born on 17 February 1966 in Colombo, Sri Lanka. She began her musical career at a young age, singing in local churches. In 1984, she made her professional debut with the song "Athuru Piditha" on the album "Sarath Sambandha". Over the following years, she released several albums and achieved significant commercial success. In 1993, she recorded the song "Nila Nila" for the soundtrack of the Sinhala film "Nila Nila". The song became a major hit and won her several awards.

In 1997, Jayamaha moved to the United Kingdom to pursue a career in music. She released her first album in the UK that year, titled "Ranee". The album received critical acclaim and spawned several hit singles, including "Ithu Ithu" and "Nandini". In 2000, she released her second UK album, titled "Innocent Melodies".
