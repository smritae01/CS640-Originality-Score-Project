The single was released in three different types: Limited Editions A and B, and a normal edition. Limited edition A came with a bonus DVD featuring the "making-of" video of the photography on the CD covers, limited edition B sported a different cover, and the first press of all three editions came with a serial number card with which to enter a draw for tickets to the single's release event.
