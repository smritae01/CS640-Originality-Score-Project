Epitaph is a German heavy metal band formed in Frankfurt, Germany in 1987. The band is considered to be one of the pioneers of the death metal genre, and has released fifteen albums since their inception. The band's style is often cited as one of the reasons why death metal became popular in the early 1990s. Epitaph's earliest releases were in the "black" style of death metal, but later albums have been more "death-thrash" oriented.
