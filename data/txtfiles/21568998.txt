Maria made landfall on the central Philippines on September 15 and weakened rapidly over the next two days. The system dissipated over the South China Sea on September 18. 

Maria was the eighteenth typhoon of the 2006 Pacific typhoon season and caused at least fifteen fatalities, ten of which were in the Philippines. Damage totaled $100 million (2006 USD).
