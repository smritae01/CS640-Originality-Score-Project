The Anton Breinl Centre for Public Health is one of Canada's leading public health institutions. The Centre's research programs focus on infectious diseases, mental health, nutrition, cancer, heart health and public policy. The Centre also offers a range of health services to the community, including family medicine, general surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics/gynecology and geriatric care. In total, the Centre employs 1,100 staff and provides services to more than 400,000 patients annually.
