Prigio is a small, wizened old man with a pot belly, a long white beard and piercing black eyes. He is also known as Il Principino, il Profeta della Saggezza, Il Mago di Borgo San Donnino, il Mago dei Ragazzi, il Maestro di via di Porta Schiavone and il Grande Mago di Rocca San Casciano.

Prigio is a mischievous old man who lives in the small Tuscan town of Borgo San Donnino with his cat, Pippo. He is known for his wisdom and magical powers. Prigio loves to play tricks on people, and he is always up for a good laugh.
