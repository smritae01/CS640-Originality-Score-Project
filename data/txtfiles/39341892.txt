American slasher film directed by David Slade, written by Matt Sazama and Burk Sharpless, and produced by Jason Blum. The film stars Patrick Wilson, Barbara Hershey, Malcolm McDowell, and John Carroll Lynch.

The film follows a group of friends who reunite for a weekend in the woods only to find themselves terrorized by an unknown assailant.


Horror Stories 2 was first announced in March 2010, with Matt Sazama and Burk Sharpless writing the script. In March 2013, it was announced that David Slade would direct the film, with Patrick Wilson, Barbara Hershey, Malcolm McDowell, and John Carroll Lynch starring. Production began in May 2013 in the United Kingdom.

The film was released on October 16, 2013 in the United States. It received mixed reviews from critics.
