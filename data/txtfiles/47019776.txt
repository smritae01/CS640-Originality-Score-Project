The city has a population of 1.8 million people. The main occupation is agriculture with crops such as paddy, cotton, groundnut, sugarcane and tobacco being the major products. There is also a small cottage industry engaged in production of textiles and handicrafts. The city has a small but growing industrial sector with production in sectors such as chemicals, pharmaceuticals, automobiles and light engineering. Tourism is also a major contributor to the economy with visitors coming for shopping and sightseeing.
