television sitcom, written by Simon Nye and directed by Andy Hamilton. The series is produced by ITV Studios and stars Philip Glenister as Mark, a successful advertising executive who is bored with his life. He decides to take a year off to travel around the world, but finds that he has to start from scratch in order to achieve his dream. 

The show was first broadcast on ITV in the United Kingdom during the 2010-2011 television season. A U.S. version of the show was announced in May 2013, and premiered on Comedy Central in October of that year. A second series was commissioned and aired on ITV in the United Kingdom during the 2014-2015 television season.
