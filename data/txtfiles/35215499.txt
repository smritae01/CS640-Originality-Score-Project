The palace is one of the most important examples of Renaissance architecture in Bologna, and is also one of the most complete and best preserved Renaissance palaces in Italy. It has been the seat of the University of Bologna since 1610.

The Palazzo Felicini is flanked by two other large Renaissance palaces, the Palazzo Della Cancelleria and the Palazzo della Pilotta.
