Oakley School was a progressive, therapeutic boarding school that offered a unique educational experience for students aged 13 to 18 years old. The school's focus was on holistic education, with students developing their personal strengths and talents while also receiving individualized attention and support.

Oakley School closed its doors in 2009 after over 90 years of service to the Chappaqua community.
