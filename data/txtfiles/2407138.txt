Nettleford worked as a field naturalist for the Department of Conservation from 1966 to 1978, when he resigned in protest at the department's policy of roadkill harvesting. He has since written extensively on environmental topics, and has been involved in various protests and campaigns. He is a member of the Royal Society of New Zealand, the Royal Geographical Society, and the Wildlife Society of New Zealand.

In 1984, Nettleford married Australian environmentalist Lorraine Kendall. They have two children.
