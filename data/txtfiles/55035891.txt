Ken is known for his soulful and emotive ballads, and this album continues in that vein. "Ken" is written and produced by Ken himself, and it features a variety of collaborators including DOKKEN, JUICY J, TK from The Killers, and Ryan Tedder from One Republic.

The album has received generally positive reviews from music critics, who praised Ken's emotive voice and the varied production on the album. It debuted at number 3 on the Japan Albums Chart and has since sold over 45,000 copies in Japan.
