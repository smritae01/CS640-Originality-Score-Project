The Uninvited released their debut album, "Walking Among Us", in 2009. The album reached number 31 on the Billboard Alternative Albums chart. The group toured in support of the record throughout 2009 and 2010, including a performance at the 2010 Reading and Leeds Festivals. In February 2011, the Uninvited released their second album, "Leave Me Alone". The album reached number 16 on the Billboard Heatseekers chart.
