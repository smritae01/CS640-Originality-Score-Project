Boylan was born in 1913 in Dublin, Ireland. She worked as a journalist for the Irish Times from 1934 to 1957, becoming its first female staff member. In 1957 she became a freelance writer and broadcaster. She wrote for the Irish Press and wrote and presented radio programmes on literature and the arts. She also wrote books on Irish authors such as James Joyce and Samuel Beckett. Boylan died in 2002 in Dublin.
